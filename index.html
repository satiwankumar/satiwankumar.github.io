About Me

As a Senior Mern Stack Developer at Royal Cyber, I develop and maintain web applications using MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js (MERN stack) to deliver scalable and high-performance solutions. I have over 5 years of experience in Full Stack development, specializing in the MERN stack along with proficiency in Next.js, Python, and SQL. My passion for innovation and problem-solving drives me to excel in crafting dynamic and responsive web experiences. I leverage my deep understanding of React.js and Redux to build user-friendly and interactive front-end interfaces, and integrate them with robust back-end services using Node.js and Express.js. I design and implement RESTful APIs using Express.js to connect front-end components with MongoDB, and ensure efficient data transfer.
